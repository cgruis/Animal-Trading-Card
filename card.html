Domestic Long-Haired Cat

The average house cat is faster than Usain Bolt.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 18 in.
  • Average Lifespan: 13-17 years
  • Habitat: Indoors

The domestic long-haired cat is a small, typically furry, carnivorous mammal. They are often valued by humans for companionship and kept as pets.
